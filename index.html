Zili Yi


School of Intelligence Science and Technology
Nanjing University
Suzhou, JS
China 342456
     

Zili Yi is a pioneering scholar-practitioner in Computer Vision/AIGC. He is well known for his academic contributions in image generation and Generative Adversarial Networks. He is the (co-)author of about 20 high-quality academic papers and the holder of 10+ US/Chinese/International patents. His publications gained 2000+ citations on google scholar. He has played essential roles in a handful of AI-based products&solutions during the period he was employed by Huawei Canada, Altumview Systems Inc. (Canada) and ByteDance (Beijing). He is currently an Associate Professor at Suzhou Campus, Nanjing University.
